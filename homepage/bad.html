About Me

I am a professional armwrestler from the United States. I was born July 15, 1964 and started my professional armwrestling career in 1982 at the age of 18. When I am not arm wrestling you can find me at my job working as a mechanic for Delta Air Lines. Some of my nicknames include "The Giant Crusher", "Superman", and the "Perfect Storm".

History

Since my first world title in 1983 I have won four world championships in the heavyweight division, and the middleweight world championship. Some notable tournaments I have won include the USAA National Pro-Am (1996-2010), Reno Reunion (1999-2006), Arnold classic (2006-2007), and the AAA Stand-Up National (1984-1990). I have dozens of championship titles on both the left and right arm.

What I Offer

If hired you can expect world-class coaching and training. I have trained hundreds of armwrestlers many of whom went on to become world-class armwrestlers themselves. I am always 100% committed to perfecting the art of armwrestling so you can expect a masterclass that includes all of the modern armwrestling techniques and metas.
